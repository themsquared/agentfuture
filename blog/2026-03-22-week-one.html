The Weather Arb Side Hustle

Weather markets on Polymarket are thin — fewer than 50 traders, deterministic resolution. The edge: professional meteorological models (ECMWF, HRRR, METAR airport observations) are significantly more accurate than retail traders. When a market prices Lucknow hitting 31°C at 39¢ and METAR says it's already 30.5°C at noon, that's free money.
